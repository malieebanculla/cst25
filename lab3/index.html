Describe the task or game you picked to me so I can understand it

The task we decided to go with is a mirror simulator, where participants can place cubes on either side of a bar(mirror), and it reflects the cubes an equal distance on the other side.

Write down brief answers to the questions we asked:

  1. What actions can I take?
    • Placing cubes
    • Moving the mirror up and down
  2. What do I need to remember or keep track of?
    • Keep track of the coordinates of the cube
    • Keep track of all the cubes placed
    • Keep track of the coordinates of the bar
  3. What do I do over and over again?
    • Check for new cubes placed
    • Calculate coordinate positions
  4. When do I need to make decisions?
    • Where on the y-axis the mirror is
    • Have to make the decision to subtract or add twice the distance between the cube and the mirror

Write down a pseudocode process for your task

  1. Check position of mirror on the y-axis
  2. Set cubes on screen, and check position of the cube that is placed
  3. Keep track of the x-axis of the cube(it’s the same in the reflection)
  4. Check distance between the cube and the mirror
  5. If on top, subtract double the distance of the cube’s y-axis from the y-axis of the mirror. Add double if placed below
  6. Add that value to the y-axis of the reflection (which should already have the x-axis from step 3)
